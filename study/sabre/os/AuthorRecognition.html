Author Recognition

The Operating Systems Resource Center goes to great pains to ensure that all authors of content contained within these pages is adequately recognized for their hard work. As such, material is only included within these pages with the express permission of the author.

In some cases, the original author cannot be determined, and is left as an unknown resource. If you have any contact information for the author of a resource, please let me know.
